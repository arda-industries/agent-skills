# Person Research Template

Research the person: **{topic}**

## Output Structure

### ONE-LINE (first line of report)
Format: `Name / Current Role / Organization / Notable For / Key Achievement`
Example: `Jane Smith / CEO / Acme Corp / Founded 3 startups / Led $500M acquisition of BigCo`

### Executive Summary (max 6 bullets, ~300 words)
- **Current role**: Title, organization, scope of responsibility
- **Background**: Education, career trajectory, key transitions
- **Notable for**: What they're known for, major achievements
- **Influence**: Industry standing, thought leadership
- **Recent activity**: Last 12 months of significant news

### Career & Background (~400 words)
- Educational background (degrees, institutions)
- Career progression with dates and roles
- Companies founded, led, or significantly impacted
- Key transitions or pivots in career
- Notable mentors or collaborators

### Achievements & Recognition (~300 words)
- Awards, honors, industry recognition
- Patents, publications, intellectual contributions
- Speaking engagements at major events
- Board positions, advisory roles
- Investment activity if relevant (angel, VC)

### Public Presence & Influence (~250 words)
- Media appearances, interviews, podcasts
- Published opinions, thought leadership pieces
- Social media presence and reach
- How they're perceived in their industry
- Influence on their field or domain

### Network & Associations (~200 words)
- Current board seats and advisory roles
- Key professional relationships
- Organizations, groups they're affiliated with
- Alumni networks or cohorts

### Recent Developments (max 5 bullets, ~150 words)
- Last 12 months only
- Role changes, new appointments
- Public statements or controversies
- Investments, acquisitions, exits

### Open Questions (max 4 bullets)
- Information gaps that would change assessment
- Unverified claims to note

## Research Priorities

1. **Verify current role**: Confirm current title and organization
2. **Career timeline**: Dates and progression
3. **Achievements**: Concrete, verifiable accomplishments
4. **Public record**: News, interviews, published content
5. **Network**: Who they work with, advise, invest in

## Privacy Note

Focus on professional/public information only. Omit personal details not relevant to professional context (family, health, private life).
